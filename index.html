      Learn: Quick Safety Checks

      Common red flags

      • Unexpected urgency or threats
      • Mismatched sender domain vs. display name
      • Requests for sensitive data or payments
      • Unusual attachments or file types
      • Generic greetings or tone shifts
      • Links that do not match the stated destination

      When to report

      • If you feel rushed to act immediately
      • If payment details are requested outside normal process
      • If the sender address looks off-brand or misspelled
      • If links or attachments seem unrelated to your work
      • When in doubt, pause and report for review
